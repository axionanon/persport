About Me

Welcome to my personal website!

I am a recent university graduate with a BA in International Economics and another BA in Art History. The current focus of my studies has been visual analysis of artwork from the 18th to 21st centuries, as well as economic theory and macro-related issues. Throughout my studies I sought to explore the interrelationships between various disciplines, while also trying to understand possible connections with emerging technologies.

I have some experience in branding and marketing, which I learned both in university and through internship experience. I enjoy coordinating and strategising, but I think I am most skilled at working with and managing others. I am currently developing my sales skills.

Beyond these subjects, I have a passionate interest in the world of web3. Though I already have a good understanding of blockchain technology in theory, I am currently in the process of deepening this on a practical level. I follow online courses in my free time, with one of my goals being learning how to effectively code smart contracts.

You can grasp my proficiency in html/css by browsing this site :)


Key interests: International art market, sales, partnerships, negotiation & strategy, web3, machine learning, applications of AI, cryptography

Contact: laconicity@proton.me
